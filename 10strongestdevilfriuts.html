One Piece: 10 Best Devil Fruits For Combat

TXN (TV Tokyo)

As the One Piece story continues, a new video game has been in development. The game, One Piece Odyssey, has many things in store for fans that will provide plenty of fun content upon its release. One of the many perks of this game is not only the brand new content but being able to use the Devil Fruit abilities in the game.

There are many different types of Devil Fruit that come in different sizes, shapes, colors, designs, and abilities. And some of these abilities that certain types provide are more suitable for combat than others.

Yami Yami no Mi

Despite its unique and colorful appearance, the Yami Yami no Mi is said to grant evil abilities. And the person who ate this fruit is none other than one of the most powerful villains in the series.

Marshall D. Teach, knwon as Blackbeard, devoured the Devil Fruit that granted him the ability to control darkness. The fruit is easily one of the most unique and terrifying amongst them, as the darkness is a void that consumes and crushes everything. The user can absorb physical matter and attack by sucking it into the void, including the opponent. And no one wants to be sucked into what is essentially a black hole.

Gura Gura no Mi

TXN (TV Tokyo)

Gura Gura no Mi is one fruit that has a fearsome reputation as being one that holds the ability to destroy the entire world. And the first person to have eaten the fruit was dubbed as one of the strongest men in the world while using the fruit's abilities.

The fruit was first eaten by Edward Newgate, otherwise known as Whitebeard. The fruit gives the user the ability to create massive vibrations and shockwaves that can travel through any medium. It also allows the user to crack or pull intangible objects such as air. However, after Whitebeard died, Blackbeard took the fruit, providing him with the powers of two Devil Fruits, making him a very dangerous opponent to go up against.

Gomu Gomu no Mi

TXN (TV Tokyo)

This fruit has a lengthy history, one that The World Government tried to erase. Despite these efforts, Monkey D. Luffy ended up eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi fruit and was able to awaken this Devil Fruit’s abilities and become one of the most powerful Devil Fruit users.

This Devil Fruit gives the user the ability to transform into the “Sun God” and gain his attributes, including the body taking on the properties of rubber, allowing their limbs to stretch out for long-range attacks. The user is also immune to blunt damage and weapons as the attacks will bounce off the skin. Many techniques come with the Devil Fruit, making the user a dangerous opponent.

Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Phoenix

TXN (TV Tokyo)

As one of the Mythical Zoan type Devil Fruits, the Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Phoenix allows the user to transform into a phoenix or a phoenix hybrid. And like with any Zoan type, the user's transformation grants them a boost in physical skills, and the fruit's user Marco makes good use of this.

While using the fruit's abilities to morph into a phoenix or a phoenix hybrid, the user can fly at great speed. The set of sharp talons provides a good amount of damage to those in kicking range, and the wings also have enough strength to restrain an opponent. It's a Devil Fruit that is great for offensive and defensive tactics in battle, making it a great asset in combat.

Ope Ope no Mi

TXN (TV Tokyo)

Aside from this fruit curing its user Trafalgar D. Water Law, the Devil Fruit Ope Ope no Mi can manifest a spherical-shaped space in which orientation, movement, and physical configuration can be manipulated in a "surgical" manner. And while this can be used to help the wounded, it can be used as a weapon in combat.

The Devil Fruit user can slice through bone and flesh without hurting the victim and without killing them. Separate pieces can be reassembled back to normal or mixed up and attached in any manner the user wants. And with its current user, his swordsmanship plays a part by using the Devil Fruit's powers with his blade to use techniques such as the Gamma Knife technique that can destroy the victim's internal organs without leaving a mark.

Ito Ito no Mi

Viz Media

Giving a gun to a ten-year-old boy that wants to kill is a bad idea, but add a Devil Fruit to that mix, and the results lead to one of the most powerful warlords known as Donquixote Doflamingo. And his Devil Fruit, Ito Ito no Mi, has served him well in combat.

Ito Ito no Mi gives the user the ability to create and control string that is extremely thin and hard to see that is not only highly durable but razor-sharp. The string is so sharp that the user can easily cut off opponents' body parts and weapons. Alongside being deadly, depending on how the abilities are used, the powers could be used to cut apart an entire country.

Pika Pika no Mi

TXN (TV Tokyo)

Admiral Kizarus has many skills, and one of the things that helped in attributing to his skills was when he ate the Pika Pika no Mi Devil Fruit. Upon consuming this Logia-type Devil Fruit, Kizarus can create, control, and transform into light, aiding him in any battle he finds himself in.

While wielding the abilities given by the Devil Fruit, the user can utilize it to create light-based attacks by allowing the user to create energy beams that can be launched at high speed from their limbs, causing massive explosions. These abilities can also blind and stun the opponent by creating quick, large bursts of light. Kizaru is known for using these abilities to create a sword made of pure light to fight his opponents.

Magu Magu no Mi

TXN (TV Tokyo)

In the earlier seasons of this long-running anime, viewers saw Luffy become enemies with quite a few different individuals. One enemy that genuinely made the future pirate king snap is Akainu, the user of the powerful Devil Fruit, Magu Magu no Mi.

Magu Magu no Mi gives the user ability to create, control, and transform into magma. Explosive properties also follow the corrosion and intense temperatures that follow magma-based attacks, such as changing the user’s fist into magma and using it during combat. A single attack can cause an explosion that will inflict tremendous damage in the surrounding area.

Mera Mera no Mi

Viz Media

Like other Devil Fruits, the Mera Mera no Mi fruit has had two users, one being the late Portgas D. Ace and now its current user Sabo. Each given the Logia-type fruit's abilities that allows for the creation, control, and ability to transform into fire.

As the current user, Sabo has inherited and obtained techniques from the Devil Fruit. One technique that the Devil Fruit has gifted Sabo with allows him to combine the fruit's power and his Ryusoken style. Sabo forms talons that he coats in Busoshoku Haki and ignites them. Using this, he slams the talons into the opponent, spins them around, and then blasts the target away with a burst of fire.

Kira Kira no Mi

YouTube

Known for gifting the user the ability to transform their body into diamond, the Kira Kira no Mi fruit powers are effective as offensive and defensive measures in combat. And the user, Jozu, otherwise known as Diamond Jozu, uses these abilities to crush his opponents in battle.

When using the abilities, the blows dealt by the user are intense. They can cause massive amounts of damage due to the density of the diamond that covers the body while acting as a shield that protects the user against powerful attacks. It can also work against long-range attacks as the diamond skin will absorb the impact.
